I am the Director of the Computational Imaging Group ([<b>CIG</b>](https://cigroup.wustl.edu/)) and the Leon and Elizabeth Janssen Associate Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering ([<b>ECE</b>](https://engineering.wisc.edu/departments/electrical-computer-engineering)) at [<b>UW–Madison</b>](https://www.wisc.edu). I am also affiliated with Wisconsin Institute for Discovery ([<b>WID</b>](https://wid.wisc.edu)). I was previously a Donald L. Snyder Associate Professor at [<b>WashU</b>](https://washu.edu/) in 2017-2025, a Visiting Professor at the [<b>ENS Paris</b>](https://www.ens.psl.eu/en) in 2024, a Visiting Faculty Researcher at [<b>Google Research</b>](https://research.google/) in 2023-2024, and a Research Scientist at Mitsubishi Electric Research Laboratories ([<b>MERL</b>](https://www.merl.com/)) in 2015-2017. I obtained my PhD in 2015 at [<b>EPFL</b>](https://epfl.ch/). I was
an Exchange Student at [<b>CMU</b>](https://www.cmu.edu/) in 2007-2008, Visiting Student Researcher at [<b>MIT</b>](https://web.mit.edu/) in 2010-2011, and Visiting Scholar at [<b>Stanford University</b>](https://www.stanford.edu/) in 2013. I was an intern at [<b>Microsoft</b>](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/) in 2009.
